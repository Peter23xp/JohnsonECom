About Us

UNSEA is a student-led organization committed to uniting Nimba students across East Africa. Our mission is to foster a strong sense of community, encourage academic and cultural collaboration, and promote peaceful coexistence among all members. We are a community of students from Nimba County, Liberia, studying in East Africa. We support each other academically, culturally, and socially, while promoting unity and leadership.
