Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, services, and policies.

How do I find my perfect fit?

We provide detailed size guides for each product category. To find your perfect fit, measure yourself according to our size guide instructions and compare your measurements with our size charts. For suits and formal wear, we recommend sizing up if you're between sizes. If you need additional assistance, our customer service team is happy to help you determine the best size for your body type.

What materials do you use in your products?

We source only the highest quality materials, including premium leather from sustainable tanneries, organic cotton, fine wool blends, and other natural fibers. Each material is carefully selected for durability, comfort, and environmental impact. For detailed information on the materials used in a specific product, please check the product description or contact our customer service team.

How do I care for my garments?

Each item comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for suits, formal wear, and leather items. For cotton garments, machine wash in cold water on a gentle cycle and hang to dry or tumble dry on low heat. Wool items should be dry cleaned or hand washed with special wool detergent. Always check the care label for the best results and to maintain the quality and longevity of your Moses pieces.

What are your shipping times and costs?

We offer several shipping options depending on your location:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): €10 or free for orders over €150
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): €20
  • International Shipping (7-14 business days): €30 or free for orders over €300

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and customs processing for international orders. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original condition with tags attached. For suits and tailored items, we provide one free alteration. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team or use the return form included with your order. Return shipping is free for exchanges, but standard return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or was shipped incorrectly.

Do you offer customization or made-to-measure services?

Yes, we offer customization and made-to-measure services for select items, particularly suits and formal wear. Our made-to-measure service includes a consultation with our expert tailors, who will take your measurements and discuss fabric and style options. Customization options include monogramming, special linings, and button selections. Please contact us to schedule an appointment or inquire about available customization options for specific products.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination and customs processing. Please note that any import duties, taxes, or customs fees are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our shipping costs. For specific information about shipping to your country, please contact our customer service team.

How do I exchange an item for a different size?

To exchange an item for a different size, please contact our customer service team within 30 days of receiving your order. For size exchanges, we offer free return shipping. Once we receive your returned item, we will ship the new size to you as soon as possible. If the new size is not available, we will issue a full refund or store credit, according to your preference.

How do I care for leather shoes and accessories?

To maintain the quality and extend the life of your leather items:

  • Clean with a soft, damp cloth and mild soap, then air dry away from direct heat
  • Apply leather conditioner every 3-6 months to prevent drying and cracking
  • Use shoe trees for footwear to maintain shape
  • Protect from water and extreme temperatures
  • For suede, use a specialized suede brush and protectant spray

We also offer a leather care kit that includes all the essentials for maintaining your leather products.
