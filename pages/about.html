Our Story

Moses Shoes & Clothing Line was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality fashion at accessible prices for the people of Liberia. What began as a small boutique has grown into one of the country's most respected clothing and footwear retailers.

Our founder, Moses Gaymore, recognized a gap in the market for premium quality clothing that was both stylish and affordable. Drawing on his passion for fashion and business acumen, he established a brand that has become synonymous with elegance, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Today, Moses Shoes & Clothing Line continues to grow, but our core values remain the same: exceptional quality, fair prices, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.
